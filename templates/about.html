About AYP

AYP is a simple, modern platform for fast crop yield predictions. Our mission is to help farmers turn data into better decisions.

What we do

We combine agricultural data, easy-to-use inputs, and intelligent forecasting so users can plan fertilizer and irrigation more effectively.

Why AYP?
